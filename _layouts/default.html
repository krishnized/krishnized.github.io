/ˈkrɪʃ.naɪzd/ v.

yat karoṣi … tat kuruṣva mad-arpaṇam — whatever you do, do that as an offering to Me. Bhagavad-gītā 9.27

mad-artham api karmāṇi kurvan siddhim avāpsyasi — work for My sake, and you will attain perfection. Bhagavad-gītā 12.10

sva-karmaṇā tam abhyarcya siddhiṁ vindati — worshipping Him through your own work, you reach perfection. Bhagavad-gītā 18.46

patraṁ puṣpaṁ phalaṁ toyaṁ … bhaktyā … aśnāmi — a leaf, a flower, fruit or water — offered with love, I accept. Bhagavad-gītā 9.26

mat-karma-kṛn mat-paramo … sa mām eti — one who works for Me, devoted to Me, comes to Me. Bhagavad-gītā 11.55
