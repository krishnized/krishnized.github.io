# Impeccable

Design context for `krishnized.com` — the home hub for one devotee's creative offerings to Krishna.

## Design Context

### Users
Gaudiya Vaishnava / Krishna-conscious practitioners and seekers looking for creative devotional
inspiration — film, books, audiobooks, music tutorials, scores, aratis, fonts, and Q&A made as
offerings. They arrive curious, often quiet, sometimes in the early-morning sadhana hours. The job:
discover and reach these offerings quickly, and feel the spirit behind them (devotion, not commerce).

### Brand Personality
Reverent, lucid, unhurried. Three words: **devotional, luminous, exact.** The voice never markets —
it offers. Sanskrit diacritics (ṛṣṇ, ā, ṅ, ḥ) are rendered correctly always; getting them right is
part of the site's integrity (it literally offers a diacritics font). Emotional goal: the calm,
awake clarity of brahma-muhurta — the hours before dawn.

### Aesthetic Direction
**Sacred dawn.** Soft off-white ground (`#fbfaf6`), twilight blue-gray ink (`#34465c` strong /
`#4f667f` body), kept near-neutral — only a whisper of warmth, and **no coloured accents on the
page** (interactions quietly darken the text and reveal an underline rather than tint). Luminous,
spacious, left-aligned and editorial, like the contents page of an illuminated book. A soft radial
"first light" pools at the top horizon and tracks the cursor. A slow verse-rotator beneath the
masthead cycles Kṛṣṇa's words on work offered to Him. Typeface: **Neue Montreal** (refined
neo-grotesque) with **Noto Sans** as the IAST-complete web fallback; hierarchy from weight/size/
spacing, not multiple faces.

- **Anti-references / NOT**: dark-mode-with-neon, glassmorphism, card grids, gradient text, stock
  "spiritual" clip-art, busy decoration, hover bullets, coloured hovers, anything that reads as
  AI-generated.
- **Theme**: light only (the dawn).

### Design Principles
1. **Offer, don't sell.** Every word and element is an offering — concrete, sincere, no marketing.
2. **Diacritics are sacred.** Sanskrit is always transliterated correctly; type must cover IAST.
3. **Light is the soul.** The dawn glow is the one signature effect — keep it, refine it, never bury it.
4. **Calm over clever.** Subtraction, breathing room, one orchestrated dawn-break reveal; no scattered gimmicks.
5. **Content / presentation split.** Content lives in `index.md` (markdown — the offerings); design
   lives in `_layouts/default.html` + `style.css`; Jekyll assembles them on GitHub Pages. To change
   the site you edit only `index.md`.
